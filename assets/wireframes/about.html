About

All teas are born from the Camellia sinensis plant. Once picked, the leaves are treated to different processes on their way to becoming Black, Oolong, Green or White tea.

On the other hand, Red and Herbal 'teas' aren't teas at all, but herbal infusions made from the Rooibos bush in the case of Red tea, or from fruits, flowers and other plants for Herbal teas.

At Twining's we offer a diverse and wide range of teas which include:

Black

Fuller bodied, with stronger flavours

Chai

Spicy and righ, we blend black tea with flavors of cinnamon, cardamom cloves and ginger

Oolong

Range from light and fragrant to dark, with a taste of toasted caramel

Green

Delicate flavours, rich with antioxidants

White

The lowest caffeine levels, the highest antioxidant levels

Roobios/Red

Made from African red bushes and filled with antioxidants

Herbal

Made from infusions of herbs, spices, fruits, and flowers
